# SAS GB Perfect Rowing Programme (4 Weeks)

## Program Levels
Two parallel programs: **Novice** and **Olympic Rower** (elite training volume)

## Intensity Zones
- **UT2 (Utilization 2):** 65-75% max HR - conversational pace
- **UT1 (Utilization 1):** 75-80% max HR
- **AT (Anaerobic Threshold):** ~85-90% max HR
- **R (Rate):** Strokes per minute (SPM)

## Technique Reference

### Catch
- Arms straight, head neutral, shoulders level
- Upper body leaning forward from hips
- Shins close to vertical (not beyond perpendicular)
- Heels may lift as needed

### Drive
- Press with legs
- Swing back through vertical
- Add arm pull last
- Hands move straight to/from flywheel
- Shoulders low, relaxed

### Finish
- Upper body leaning back slightly
- Core support engaged
- Legs extended
- Handle held lightly below ribs
- Shoulders low, wrists/grip relaxed

### Recovery
- Extend arms until straight
- Lean from hips toward flywheel
- Hands clear knees
- Knees bend, slide forward gradually
- Return to catch position

## Training Structure Summary

### Novice Progression
- **Week 1-2:** Build consistency (2-3 sessions, 10-20 min)
- **Week 3-4:** Increase volume (20-30 min), add variety (cross-training, split work)
- **Total weekly time:** 40-90 min
- **Focus:** Technique, aerobic base, exercise habit

### Olympic Rower Characteristics
- **Volume:** 130-156 km/week
- **Sessions:** 12-15/week (often 2-3/day)
- **Distribution:** ~85-90% UT2/UT1, 10-15% threshold/high-intensity
- **Structure:** AM/PM doubles with evening weights/core
- **Focus:** High aerobic volume, technique at low rates, periodic intensity (Week 4 testing)

## Key Features
- **Low-rate work (R18-19):** Builds efficiency, aerobic base
- **Structured intervals:** 2km, 6km, 500m pieces
- **Cross-training:** Cycling for active recovery/additional volume
- **Strength/Core:** 4-6 sessions/week (Olympic)
- **Week 4:** Testing/deload week (VO₂ max test, reduced volume)
