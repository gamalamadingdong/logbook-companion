## Catch

- Arms straight; head neutral; shoulders level
- Upper body leaning forward from the hips
- Shins as close to vertical as is comfortable
- Shins should not go beyond perpendicular
- Heels may lift as needed

![](_page_1_Picture_12.jpeg)

#### Drive

- Press with your legs
- Swing your back through the vertical
- Finally add the arm pull
- Hands move straight to and from flywheel
- Shoulders low and relaxed

![](_page_1_Picture_19.jpeg)

### Finish

- Upper body leaning back slightly
- Good support from the core muscles
- Legs are extended
- Handle held lightly below your ribs
- Shoulders low with wrists and grip relaxed

![](_page_1_Picture_26.jpeg)

### Recovery

- Extend your arms until straight
- Lean from the hips towards the flywheel
- Hands clear your knees
- Knees bend and gradually slide forward
- Return to the catch position
