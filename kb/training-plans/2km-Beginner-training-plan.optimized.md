# Beginner 2k Training Plan (8 Weeks)

## Target Audience
New to indoor rowing or regular exercise. Builds to confidently rowing 2000m.

## Setup Guidelines

| Element | Guidance |
|---|---|
| **Technique** | Learn basics: legs → body → arms (drive), arms → body → legs (recovery). Count 2 on drive, 3 on recovery. |
| **Equipment** | Monitor at eye level. Foot strap across ball of foot. Damper ≤4 (Concept2). |
| **Warm-up/Cool-down** | 5 min warm-up before. 5 min cool-down + stretch after. |
| **Stroke Rate (SPM)** | 18-24 while learning. Focus on consistency over speed. |
| **Split/Pace** | Time to row 500m. Keep consistent - improves with technique/fitness. |

## Intensity Zones

| Intensity | Feel | Conversation | Guide Rate | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Low** | Comfortable | Full conversation | 18-22 | Sustainable long |
| **Medium** | Heart rate up | Short sentences | 22-26 | Not long periods |
| **High** | Breathing hard | Difficult | 26+ | Short bursts only |

## Weekly Progression

### Week 1: Adaptation & Technique
**Focus:** Get comfortable on machine, develop consistent stroke, basic fitness

- **Session 1:** 1 min low, 1 min rest × 5
- **Session 2:** 5 min low, 3 min rest × 2
- **Fitness Boost:** 10 min jog/walk (1 min on/off)

### Week 2: Build Duration
**Focus:** Increase time exercising, smooth rowing with powerful drive + slow recovery

- **Session 1:** 2 min low, 1 min rest × 5
- **Session 2:** 5 min low, 3 min rest × 3
- **Fitness Boost:** 15 min jog/walk (1 min on/off)

### Week 3: Add Intensity
**Focus:** Longer duration, higher intensity, consistent stroke rate/split

- **Session 1:** 500m medium, 2 min rest × 4 (note times)
- **Session 2:** 10 min row (5 min low, 5 min medium)
- **Fitness Boost:** 20 min jog/walk (1 min on/off)

### Week 4: Distance Focus
**Focus:** More medium work, distance-based (work harder = finish quicker), technique control

- **Session 1:** 1000m medium, 5 min rest × 2
- **Session 2:** 15 min row (3 min low, 3 min med, 3 low, 3 med, 3 low)
- **Fitness Boost:** 20 min jog/walk (2 min on/1 min off)

### Week 5: First 2k
**Focus:** Comfortable with current level, complete first 2000m at medium

- **Session 1:** 30 strokes low + 10 strokes medium burst × 5
- **Session 2:** 2000m row - note time
  - *Target pace calculation:* Add Week 3 4×500m times + 20 sec, divide by 4
  - *Example:* 2:30 + 2:40 + 2:40 + 2:38 + 20 sec = 10:48 = 2:42/500m target
- **Fitness Boost:** 20 min jog/walk (3 min on/1 min off)

### Week 6: High-Intensity Introduction
**Focus:** Short high-intensity pieces, build fitness, quick recovery ability

- **Session 1:** 1 min high, 1 min rest × 6
- **Session 2:** 500m medium, 2 min rest × 4 (note times, compare to Week 3)
- **Fitness Boost:** 25 min jog/walk (3 min on/1 min off)

### Week 7: Varied Workouts
**Focus:** Control technique when pushing hard, maintain form when tired

- **Session 1:** 1000m medium (every other min: 10 stroke high burst), 5 min rest × 2
- **Session 2:** 10 min row (2 min low, 2 med, 1 high, 2 med, 3 low), 5 min rest × 2
- **Fitness Boost:** 25 min jog/walk (4 min on/1 min off)

### Week 8: Final Test
**Focus:** Powerful drive + slow recovery. Compare 500m to Week 3 & 6

- **Session 1:** 5 min medium, 3 min rest × 4
- **Session 2:** 500m medium, 1000m medium, 500m high (2 min rest between each)
- **Fitness Boost:** 30 min jog/walk (4 min on/1 min off)

## Post-Program
Ready for 2k challenges! Consider:
- Indoor rowing events
- Charity challenges
- Intermediate training plan
- Team competitions

## Key Success Factors
- Consistent session completion (2-3×/week minimum)
- Proper technique before speed
- Gradual intensity progression
- Cross-training for variety
- Track times to see improvement
