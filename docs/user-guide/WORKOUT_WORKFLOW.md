### Understanding Workout Structure (RWN Breakdown)

Before you row, understand what you're about to do:

**Example:** `[w]10:00 + 8x500m@2k+5/1:00r + [c]5:00`

**Breaking it down:**
- `[w]10:00` = 10 minute warmup
- `+` = followed by (no extra rest)
- `8x500m` = 8 intervals of 500 meters
- `@2k+5` = at 5 seconds slower than your 2k PR pace
- `/1:00r` = with 1 minute rest between intervals
- `+` = followed by
- `[c]5:00` = 5 minute cooldown

**Total time estimate:** ~45 minutes (10 + 8Ã—2 + 7Ã—1 + 5)

**Pro tip:** The template detail page shows:
- Estimated duration
- Total distance
- Work vs. rest breakdown
- Guidance targets (if specified)

## Advanced Workflows: Power User Features

### Live Sessions (Experimental)

**What it is:** Program your PM5 directly from the browser via Bluetooth.

**How it works:**
1. Open **Live Sessions**
2. Create a new session
3. Select or create a workout template
4. Connect to your PM5 via Bluetooth
5. Click **"Send to PM5"**
6. Workout appears on your monitor, ready to row

**Benefits:**
- No manual PM5 programming
- Workout structure matches your template exactly
- Automatic sync after completion

**Note:** This feature is experimental and requires:
- PM5 monitor (Bluetooth-enabled)
- Chrome/Edge browser (Web Bluetooth support)
- Proximity to the erg

### Group Workouts (Coach View)

**What it is:** Coach can see real-time data from multiple athletes.

**Use cases:**
- Team interval sessions

- Synchronized workouts

**How it works:**

**Benefits:**
- Real-time monitoring

## Common Questions

**Q: What if I forget to sync?**  
A: Concept2 stores your workouts indefinitely. Sync anytime, even weeks later.

**Q: Can I edit a workout after syncing?**  
A: Yes. You can add notes, adjust metrics, or change the linked template.

**Q: What if the automatic matching is wrong?**  
A: You can manually change the linked template on the workout detail page.

**Q: Can I log on-water workouts?**  
A: Yes! Use manual entry with RWN. You won't have split data, but you'll still get template matching and trend tracking.

**Q: How do I track non-rowing workouts?**  
A: RWN supports other modalities: `Run: 5000m`, `Bike: 30:00`, `Other: Strength training`

**Q: Can I plan future workouts?**  
A: Yes! Create a workout with a future date. It won't have performance data yet, but it's in your calendar.
